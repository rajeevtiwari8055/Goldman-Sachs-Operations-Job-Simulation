## <span id="project-overview">📌 Project Overview</span>

This repository showcases my work from the **Goldman Sachs – Operations Job Simulation**, offered through **Forage**.

The program simulates real-world responsibilities of an **Operations Analyst in Wealth Management**, focusing on transaction management, trade settlement, and operational risk handling.

This simulation provided hands-on exposure to how financial institutions ensure accurate, timely, and compliant execution of high-value transactions, especially for ultra-high-net-worth (UHNW) clients.

---

## <span id="program-context">🏢 Program Context</span>

- **Organization:** Goldman Sachs  
- **Platform:** Forage  
- **Domain:** Operations & Wealth Management  
- **Duration:** 1–2 Hours (Self-paced)  
- **Assessment:** No grades, no exams  
- **Outcome:** Completion Certificate  

Goldman Sachs is a leading global investment bank known for excellence in client service, operational efficiency, and risk management.

---

## <span id="why-this-simulation">💡 Why This Job Simulation</span>

This simulation offers a **risk-free, real-world experience** of working in Operations at Goldman Sachs.

It helps learners to:

- Understand transaction workflows in financial services  
- Identify and resolve trade-related issues  
- Learn cross-functional collaboration  
- Apply risk management and compliance principles  
- Build confidence for real-world roles and interviews

## <span id="task-one">📊 Task One: Foundations of Operations</span>

**Time Required:** 30–60 Minutes  

### What I Did

- Explored the role of an Operations Analyst  
- Applied operational thinking to real-life scenarios  
- Understood transaction processes and workflows  
- Identified areas for process improvement  

### What I Learned

- Fundamentals of wealth management operations  
- Importance of efficiency and accuracy  
- Role of operations in client satisfaction  

---

## <span id="task-two">💰 Task Two: UHNW Transaction Management</span>

### What I Did

- Analyzed financial reports to identify trade issues  
- Detected delays in fund transfers and settlements  
- Identified discrepancies in transaction data  
- Collaborated with Trading, Compliance, and IT teams  
- Prepared a Trade Fail Report with risk assessment  

### Key Learnings

- Trade settlement lifecycle  
- Risk identification and mitigation  
- Regulatory compliance importance  
- Cross-functional collaboration in operations
